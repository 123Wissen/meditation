Our Mission

To provide a peaceful, intuitive, and reliable meditation timer that helps people cultivate mindfulness and inner peace in their daily lives. We believe that regular meditation practice can transform lives, and we're here to make that journey easier.

Our Vision

To become the go-to meditation companion for practitioners of all levels, helping them develop and maintain a consistent meditation practice. We envision a world where mindfulness is accessible to everyone, anywhere, anytime.
