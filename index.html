Getting Started

Choose Your Timer

Select from three timer types: Count Down for a simple countdown, Duration to track elapsed time, or Interval for alternating meditation and rest periods.

Set Your Time

Enter your desired meditation time in minutes. For the Interval timer, also set your rest period and number of cycles.

Using the Timer

Start & Focus

Click the Start button and begin your meditation practice. The timer will display your progress and play gentle bell sounds at intervals.

Customize Settings

Toggle sound on/off or use fullscreen mode for a distraction-free experience using the buttons at the bottom of the screen.

Mental Benefits

Reduced Stress & Anxiety

Regular meditation practice has been shown to lower cortisol levels, reducing stress and anxiety while promoting a sense of calm.

Improved Focus

Meditation enhances concentration and attention span, helping you stay more present and focused throughout your day.

Physical Benefits

Better Sleep

Regular meditation can help regulate sleep patterns, making it easier to fall asleep and improving overall sleep quality.

Enhanced Well-being

Meditation promotes emotional health, increases self-awareness, and can generate kindness through loving-kindness meditation.
